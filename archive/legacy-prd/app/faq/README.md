<p>Q：</p>
<p>What types of cards does AIX Pay offer?</p>
<p>A：</p>
<p>AIX Pay offers two card types: a Physical Card for everyday in-store use and a Virtual Card for online and digital payments. Each card has its own unique number and works just like a debit card, letting you spend directly from your stablecoin balance.</p>
<p>Getting started is simple, just apply for an AIX Pay card, deposit your preferred stablecoin into your AIX Pay account, and you're ready to spend.</p>
<p>For added convenience, you can easily add your card to Google Pay or Apple Pay and enjoy seamless tap-to-pay transactions wherever you go.</p>
<p>Q：</p>
<p>Who is eligible to open an AIX Pay account?</p>
<p>A：</p>
<p>You're eligible as long as you can provide a valid passport and a proof of address document that matches your country of residence.</p>
<p>Q：</p>
<p>How do I apply for a AIX Pay card?<br />
A：</p>
<p>Applying for your AIX Pay card is simple! Just follow these steps based on either a virtual card (for instant online usage) or a physical card (for in-store and ATM withdrawals).</p>
<p>Virtual Card</p>
<p>Download the AIX Pay app and create your account.</p>
<p>Head to the "AIX Pay Card" tab and tap "Get Card".</p>
<p>Select Virtual Card and choose your favorite design.</p>
<p>Link your preferred stablecoin and tap "Apply Card".</p>
<p>Review your account details, tap "Checkout" and pay the USD $5 application fee.</p>
<p>You're all set! Your virtual card will be ready to use in just a few minutes.</p>
<p>Physical Card</p>
<p>Download the AIX Pay app and create your account</p>
<p>Head to the "AIX Pay Card" tab and tap "Get Card".</p>
<p>Select Physical Card and choose your favorite design.</p>
<p>Link your preferred stablecoin and tap "Apply Card".</p>
<p>Review your mailing address, tap "Checkout", and pay the USD $10 application fee.</p>
<p>You're all set! You can expect your physical within a few days.</p>
<p>Tips:</p>
<p>- You can add your virtual and physical cards to digital wallets like Google Pay, Apple Pay, or Samsung Pay for seamless tap-to-pay purchases.</p>
<p>- Make sure you have enough balance in card’s default currency.</p></td>

<p>Q: How do I activate my card once I receive it?</p>
<p>A:</p>
<p>Your virtual card doesn't need activation! Once your application is approved, it's automatically active and ready to use right away.</p>
<p>Tip: You can add your virtual cards to digital wallets like Google Pay, Apple Pay, or Samsung Pay for seamless tap-to-pay purchases.</p>
<p>Q: Why can't I pay the card fee even though I have enough balance?<br />
A:</p>
<p>Your card fee can only be paid using the stablecoin that matches your card’s default currency. At this time, payments with other stablecoins aren’t supported.</p>
<p>What you can do:</p>
<p>- Top up the stablecoin that matches your card’s default currency, or</p>
<p>- Update your card’s default currency to match the stablecoin balance you’d like to use</p></td>
